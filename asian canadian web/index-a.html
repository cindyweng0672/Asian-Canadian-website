William “Bill” Gun Chong

Achievement

Life Story / Contribution/ Achievement/ Reference

William "Bill" Gun Chong is the only Chinese Canadian who was awarded the British Empire Medal which is the highest honor given by the British government to non-British citizens. He also got the 1939-1945 Star which is a military campaign medal that the British government instituted on 8 July 1943 to award British and Commonwealth forces for service in the Second World War, the Pacific Star, which is an award instituted by the British government to award people who served in the Pacific Campaign from 1941 to 1945, and the War Medal which is a medal to award all full-time people of the Armed Forces and Merchant Marines for serving for 28 days between 03 September 1939 and 02 September 1945.
